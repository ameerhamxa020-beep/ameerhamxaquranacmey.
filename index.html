About the Academy

A Home for Quran Learning in Tarnol

Ameer Hamxa Quran Academy was founded to make authentic, well-taught Quran education accessible to every home — whether a child is beginning with Noorani Qaida or an adult wants to perfect their Tajweed. Our teachers hold recognized qualifications from the KIU and Wifaq boards, and every class blends correct recitation with the manners (adaab) of the Quran.

We offer flexible online classes for students anywhere, as well as in-person classes at our Tarnol, Islamabad center — with separate, comfortable arrangements for male and female students.

  • Qualified male & female teachers
  • One-on-one and group class options
  • Flexible timing for students & working adults
  • Regular progress reports for parents
Our Courses

Courses for Every Stage of Learning

From the first letters of Noorani Qaida to complete memorization and deep understanding — a clear path for every student.

01

Noorani Qaida

The foundational course teaching correct Arabic letter recognition and pronunciation — the essential first step for every new student.

02

Nazra Quran

Learning to read the Quran fluently and correctly with proper pronunciation, at a pace suited to the student.

03

Hifz-ul-Quran

Complete memorization of the Holy Quran with structured daily targets, revision cycles and one-on-one guidance.

04

Tajweed

Mastering the rules of correct Quranic recitation — makhraj, madd and the science of beautiful, accurate tilawat.

05

Tafseer

Understanding the meaning, context and message of the Quran under qualified scholarly guidance.

06

Islamic Studies

Core beliefs, fiqh, seerah and everyday Islamic practice, taught in a simple and practical way.

07

Arabic Language

Building reading, writing and comprehension skills in Arabic to connect directly with the Quran and Islamic texts.
